# LeadScope Quick Start Guide

## Discover and Export in 5 Steps

### 1️⃣ Go to Discover Page
Navigate to **Discover** in the menu

### 2️⃣ Select Your Criteria
- Choose **Prefecture** (e.g., Αττικής)
- Choose **Municipality** (e.g., Αθήνα)
- Choose **Industry** (e.g., Restaurants)

### 3️⃣ Click Search
Click the **Search** button. The system will:
- Check local database first
- If no results, automatically fetch from GEMI registry

### 4️⃣ Wait for Completion
- Discovery runs automatically
- A completion modal appears when done
- Shows number of businesses found

### 5️⃣ Export Your Data
**Option A**: Click **"Proceed to Export"** in the completion modal

**Option B**: 
1. Go to **Exports** page
2. Click **"Add Export"**
3. Select your dataset
4. Choose **Excel (.xlsx)** format
5. Click **"Create Export"**

### 6️⃣ Download
1. Go to **Exports** page
2. Find your completed export
3. Click **"Download"** button
4. Open the `.xlsx` file in Excel or Google Sheets

---

## Tips

✅ **Excel format** is recommended for large datasets  
✅ Exports process in the background - you can continue working  
✅ Datasets are automatically saved - you can export them anytime  
✅ Check the **Exports** page to see when your export is ready
